====== Stars ======
Stars formulate and grow over time. They litter the Neveah with life, albeit scarcely.

The people far below look up for light, the beacon of higher power, might. The source thought impossible to obtain - which so resulted in worship, and endless respect.

But scholars know not restraint.

With the knowledge that comes with power, growing colonies expand their priorities to the future. The master scholar of [[Gart]], [[Adyna Ccri]] shaped the path forward with the invention of the [[Seren]]. Her instrument of power harnesses the power of fallen stars to bring down others.
With this device, the power of stars was made available to the people, This massive divulgence in understanding of the world brought revolutionary change to the structure of peoples beliefs.
